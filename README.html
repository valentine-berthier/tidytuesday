Tips to criticize

The Trifecta Checkup involves only three investigations:

What is the QUESTION? What does the DATA say? What does the VISUAL say?

. * Message: Is the main point of the visualization clear? Does the visualization effectively communicate the intended message? * Visual: Are the design elements visually appealing and easy to understand? Do they enhance the viewers understanding of the data? * Data: Is the data accurate and relevant? Are any potential biases or limitations in the data addressed?

  • draw the viewers attention to the most important information.
